Roadmap

One personal mission, carried by two companies. Each phase funds and de-risks the next.

  1. Educate — now

    Train the talent the industry doesn't have yet

    Burmese-language game development education through GMM's academy — 100+ developers trained, with free publishing for students who finish what they start.

  2. Publish — now

    Put Myanmar-made games on world storefronts

    Five titles published as founder — from Witching Nights: Tower Defense on Google Play to Anominal and Them's Tale on Steam — proving local developers can ship to a global standard.

  3. Prove — next

    Make engagement an engineering discipline

    Through NPI, serious games and gamification that move measurable business KPIs — the service engine that funds original creative IP without surrendering it.

  4. Elevate — beyond

    Make Myanmar a serious player in the global game industry

    Original titles on global platforms, a publishing bridge international studios trust, and a generation of developers who never had to teach themselves alone.
